LibraryOne

The LibraryOne project addresses the common issue faced by college students who need to carry physical library cards to access books. Our solution introduces a Virtual Library Card that simplifies this process. With LibraryOne, students can use their phone or email to receive a One-Time Password (OTP). Simply enter the OTP to verify your identity and gain instant access to the books you need, all without the hassle of carrying a physical card.
